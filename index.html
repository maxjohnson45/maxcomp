Everyday Code

Code affects our everyday life because technology is so prominent in everyday society, and every website or app we interact with is created by several lines of code. After completing the activity and tracking my screen time for five days straight, I saw that I spend way more time on social media than I thought I did. Since I was tracking over labor day weekend, I had a lot more free time than I would have during a normal school day. The data has encouraged me to find other more beneficial ways to spend my free time and be mindful of when I am pointlessly scrolling on Tiktok.
